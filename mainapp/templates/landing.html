Frequently asked questions

No. Your QR opens a public web page with your ad that works on any phone.

Yes, you can update price, photos, and details at any time.

Is my phone number exposed?

You decide whether to show a call button, message form, or both.

No commission. Plans are flat monthly fees where applicable.
