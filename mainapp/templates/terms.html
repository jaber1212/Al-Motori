Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how motoria collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile applications, websites, and related services for creating and browsing vehicle sale advertisements. Some listings can be linked to a QR code that opens a public web page with the ad’s content when scanned.

Summary: We collect account data, ad content, and limited analytics. We use it to operate, secure, and improve motoria; to display and promote listings; and to provide QR-based experiences.

Information We Collect

  • Account & Profile: name, phone number, password hash, and settings you provide.
  • Ad Content: vehicle details, price, location area or city (if you add it), photos/videos, and associated metadata.
  • Usage & Device: app and web interactions, device type, language, approximate location derived from IP, and identifiers such as push notification tokens.
  • QR Interactions: when someone scans a QR code that opens an ad’s public web page, we may log the request including IP address, user-agent, time, and referrer.
  • Communications: messages you send via in-app forms or support channels.
  • Cookies & Similar: limited cookies/local storage for session management and analytics on the web experience.

How We Use Information

  • Provide and maintain motoria, including creating, displaying, and promoting your listings.
  • Enable QR-linked experiences so that scans open the correct ad page.
  • Protect users and platform integrity, detect fraud, enforce our Terms, and prevent misuse.
  • Measure performance, understand usage, and improve features and relevance.
  • Communicate with you about your account, safety notices, updates, and support.
  • Comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests.

How We Share Information

  • Public Content: Ad pages are public by nature; information you include in a listing (e.g., photos, price) is visible to anyone who opens the page or scans its QR code.
  • Service Providers: We use vendors for hosting, storage, analytics, notifications, and security. They access data only to perform services for us.
  • Legal & Safety: We may disclose information to comply with the law, enforce our policies, or protect rights, safety, and property.
  • Business Transfers: In a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, data may be transferred subject to this Policy.

QR Codes & Analytics

When a QR code is scanned, we may capture technical information (e.g., device/user-agent, IP address, time, approximate location from IP, and referrer) to route the request, prevent abuse, and understand engagement with your ad. This information is processed under legitimate interests to provide and secure the service.

Data Security & Retention

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure. We retain information for as long as necessary to operate motoria, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Your Choices & Rights

  • Access, update, or delete certain account and listing information from within the app or by contacting support.
  • Control notifications in your device settings and app preferences.
  • Request a copy of your data or object to certain processing where applicable by law.

Contact

If you have privacy questions or requests, please reach out through the in-app support form.

This Privacy Policy is provided for general informational purposes and does not constitute legal advice.
